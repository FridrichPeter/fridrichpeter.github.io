About Me

As an experienced GIS Specialist with a passion for coding and learning new technologies, I have a wide range of skills in geospatial tools such as ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, FME, qGIS, PostgreSQL, LAStools for Lidar data, and GDAL/OGR. With my expertise in Python and arcpy library, I can automate tasks and optimize workflows. I also have experience in web development, building websites with HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery. My experience includes database management, spatial data modeling, ETL, and batch data processing using Python or FME. I have worked on projects related to transportation infrastructure, flood risk management, and spatial planning aimed at reducing the adverse consequences of floods on human health, the environment, cultural heritage, and economic activity. In my previous project, I used mobile mapping technology (Trimble) and Lidar (Velodyne) to perform clearance analysis for roads, bridges, tunnels, and other transportation infrastructure elements. As a driven, highly motivated, and proactive individual, I can work autonomously or collaborate with a team to achieve results. I am goal-oriented, and my positive attitude helps me overcome any challenges that arise during the project. here
